The Best Movies According to Sharath

My top 3 movies of all time.

Talk to Me (2022) :

One of the most horrifying and skin-crawling horror films in recent times. A group of friends conjures dead spirits within them to experience a high.

Ready Player One (2018) :

An entertaining film packed with pop culture references. Set in a future world where people live in a virtual reality called "Oasis," the protagonist competes in a contest for its ownership.

Tron Legacy (2010) :

A sequel to the legendary 80s movie "Tron." Sam Flynn, son of a brilliant scientist, gets pulled into a digital world his father created and helps its inhabitants by defeating a dictator.
